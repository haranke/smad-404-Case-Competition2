Featured Work

The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes details the Tenth Annual Hunger Games as Coriolanus Snow turns 18 and worries about his future. The Snows, once wealthy and powerful, are practically no more after the deaths of Coriolanus's mother and military father, the latter killed during the districts' failed rebellion.
